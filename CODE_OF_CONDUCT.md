# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone who contributes to or engages with the ASPS™ project.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Respectful and constructive communication
- Graceful acceptance of feedback
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project
- Empathy toward other contributors

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or imagery
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to [contact@beunec.com](mailto:contact@beunec.com) or [support@beunec.com](mailto:support@beunec.com). All reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly and confidentially.

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and to temporarily or permanently ban contributors for inappropriate behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — including GitHub Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions, and any external channels representing the project.
